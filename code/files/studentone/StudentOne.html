。・★ Octo Pattern。・★

Octopus Pattern Body: In first color: Make a magic circle Round 1: 6 sc in magic circle Round 2: 2 sc in each stitch around Round 3: sc 1; 2 sc in next stitch Round 4: sc 2; 2 sc in next stitch Round 5: sc 3; 2 sc in next stitch Round 6: sc 4; 2 sc in next stitch Round 7: sc 5; 2 sc in next stitch Round 8-17: sc in each stitch around Fasten off Repeat all steps in second color On one dome, sew a smile on and attach eyes On the other dome, sew an angry face and attach eyes Tentacles: In first color: make 6 Make a magic circle Round 1: 6 sc in magic circle Round 2: 2 sc in each stitch Round 3: sc 1; 2 sc in next stitch Round 4: sc 2; 2 sc in next stitch Repeat all steps 6 times in second color Finishing: •sew two circles together then attach to body .repeat 6 times .

★ Small Whale Pattern ★

Starting with main body color R1: 6sc in mr (6) R2: 6inc (12) R3: (1sc, inc) *6 (18) R4: sc all around (18) R5: *now we are going to make the tail and fins* (ch 3 and sl in the first chain you made) I'm going to call this fin, sc in the next st then make another fin, sc 5, make a fin, sc 7, make a fin, sc 5 but change to belly color on the last sc.*remember to be using the belly color* R6: Now you're going to want to sc around the fins and

only go into the sc you made (18) R7: (1sc, invdec) *6 (12) *now take some time and add eyes in the stitch next to the two front fins. and start to stuff R8: invdec every st finish stuffing and FO
